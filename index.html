About Stellar

What is Stellar?

Stellar is an open-source, decentralized payment protocol designed for fast, low-cost cross-border transactions. Founded in 2014, it enables anyone to build financial products that connect people everywhere.

Soroban smart contracts

Stellar's Soroban platform brings Rust-based smart contracts to the network, enabling DeFi, NFTs, and complex financial instruments — all with the same low fees and fast finality.

Federated consensus

Stellar uses the Federated Byzantine Agreement (FBA) model — transactions confirm in 3–5 seconds without energy-intensive mining, making it one of the greenest blockchains available.

Real-world adoption

Used by MoneyGram, Circle (USDC), and dozens of central banks for CBDC pilots. Stellar processes millions of transactions monthly across 200+ countries.
